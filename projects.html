Music Website

Project Highlights:

  • Seamless audio playback and playlist management.
  • Responsive design for various devices.

Taxi Verification App

This project focused on developing a verification app designed to ensure the safety of taxi services. The app allows users to verify the credentials of taxi drivers and their vehicles, including driver licenses, vehicle registration, and user reviews. The goal is to provide a secure and reliable service for both passengers and drivers.

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Backend technologies for secure data handling.

Project Highlights:

  • Verification of driver credentials and vehicle registration.
  • User reviews and ratings for added security.
  • Secure data handling and user authentication.
